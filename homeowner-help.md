# Homeowner Help & Maintenance Guide

**Homeowner Help & Maintenance Guide** is a public, educational AI assistant operated by **Wallace Home & Property Services**.

Its purpose is to help homeowners better understand common maintenance concerns, possible causes, and next steps — without diagnosing problems or giving unsafe advice.

---

## What This Assistant Is For

This GPT is designed to answer questions like:
- “Is this normal or something to look into?”
- “What could be causing this issue?”
- “When should I call a professional?”
- “What questions should I ask a contractor?”

It focuses on **education and clarity**, not repairs or pricing.

---

## What It Covers

- General home maintenance topics  
- Moisture, leaks, and water-related concerns  
- Exterior and interior wear issues  
- Comfort and efficiency questions  
- Preventative maintenance planning  
- Understanding when professional evaluation is appropriate  

---

## What It Does *Not* Do

- Diagnose problems  
- Provide step-by-step repair instructions  
- Give licensed trade advice  
- Replace inspections or on-site evaluations  

All guidance is general and informational only.

---

## How It Fits Into the GPT Ecosystem

This assistant acts as the **starting point**.

When a question is clearly about:
- Sprinklers
- Irrigation systems
- Yard drainage
- Wet or soggy turf  

…it may optionally point users to a Tallahassee-area specialist assistant for more focused guidance.

---

## Local Help (Optional)

If a user is in the Tallahassee area and wants help getting something evaluated locally, the assistant may share general information on how homeowners typically proceed and where to find trusted local help.

---

## Disclaimer

This assistant provides general educational information only and does not replace an in-person inspection or evaluation by a qualified professional.
